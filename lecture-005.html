005 The honest way to talk to users

Planned · TBD · planned

Talking to users is not asking what features they want. It is finding what they already do and what hurts, then mapping your product to that reality.

Key takeaways

  • Users do not speak in product requirements
  • Look for workarounds, not opinions
  • Ask for examples, then ask for the last time
  • A user who pays is the clearest sentence

Q and A notes

  • What questions work best? Tell me about the last time you did this.
  • How do you avoid leading them? You do not pitch. You listen for pain and priorities.
  • When do you demo? After you can repeat their problem back to them.
