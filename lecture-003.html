003 Why your product is not ready yet

TBD · David Morales, Founder (Runbook) · draft

Most products fail in one moment: the first time a user is confused. Fix the first week before you expand the roadmap.

Key takeaways

  • Confusion breaks trust faster than missing features
  • Support is the real product
  • Onboarding beats roadmap
  • Fix the first week

Q and A notes

  • What do you cut first? Everything that does not make the first user successful.
  • What should you measure? Time to first success and time to first question.
  • How do you decide what to build next? Build what removes repeated friction.
